**Black Wednesday** was a [[Speculative Attack]] on the Great British pound that occurred on September 16th, 1992. Fears over the Brit's ability to to deal with the ongoing recession while under ERM restrictions led to mass sell offs of the pound sterling that were further enabled by the liberalized capital controls of EMU stage 1.
